Steve Blank: Acting on Customer Discovery

Customer Discovery first Steps

The first step in trying to learn from the market is having an opinion about who your market actually is. Your job is to think through the kinds of people who have the problem you are interested in solving.

  • The typical customer you envision if you get traction with your idea
  • Your early adopter, i.e. the people who will take a chance on your product before anyone else (If you cannot get early adopters, you cannot move on. Early adopters are usually people who feel a pain point acutely, or love to try new products and services.)
  • Critical partners for distribution, fulfillment, or other parts of your business
  • A hospital management system has to think about the hospital administrator who will buy their software and the actual hospital workers who would use it
